1""" 

2Colorbars are a visualization of the mapping from scalar values to colors. 

3In Matplotlib they are drawn into a dedicated `~.axes.Axes`. 

4 

5.. note:: 

6 Colorbars are typically created through `.Figure.colorbar` or its pyplot 

7 wrapper `.pyplot.colorbar`, which use `.make_axes` and `.Colorbar` 

8 internally. 

9 

10 As an end-user, you most likely won't have to call the methods or 

11 instantiate the classes in this module explicitly. 

12 

13:class:`ColorbarBase` 

14 The base class with full colorbar drawing functionality. 

15 It can be used as-is to make a colorbar for a given colormap; 

16 a mappable object (e.g., image) is not needed. 

17 

18:class:`Colorbar` 

19 On top of `.ColorbarBase` this connects the colorbar with a 

20 `.ScalarMappable` such as an image or contour plot. 

21 

22:func:`make_axes` 

23 Create an `~.axes.Axes` suitable for a colorbar. This functions can be 

24 used with figures containing a single axes or with freely placed axes. 

25 

26:func:`make_axes_gridspec` 

27 Create a `~.SubplotBase` suitable for a colorbar. This function should 

28 be used for adding a colorbar to a `.GridSpec`. 

29"""
